E-Commerce Web Application

Problem Statement

Small businesses struggle to establish an online presence due to high costs of custom e-commerce solutions. Many lack the technical knowledge to set up and manage online stores, resulting in lost revenue opportunities and limited market reach.

Solution

Event Management System

Problem Statement

Managing events manually is difficult and time-consuming. Tasks like event registration, booking venues, managing participants, and handling payments often lead to confusion and errors. There is no proper centralized system to organize all event-related activities in one place. This results in poor coordination, miscommunication, and inefficient management of events.

Solution
